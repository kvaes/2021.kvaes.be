A little about me

Been working in information technology related roles for more than 20 years. Currently active as a Business Developer on Azure Application Development for Belgium & Luxemburg based customers. Prior to that a program manager for the Azure Storage engineering team leading the Autonomous Driving effort in Europe. Where initially joining Microsoft as a Cloud Solution Architect in 2017

Been blogging since 2007 and also active as a public speaker at various community events. These efforts earned me the "Microsoft Most Valuable Professional"-award in the Azure category.

Prior to Microsoft, active as a consultant since 2011. My first tenure was for a system integrator & managed services provider, where my role started out as an infra/enterprise architect, and evolved to a business development manager for cloud & outsourcing. In this role, the key aspect was driving the largest and strategic datacenter migration & outsourcing projects. In the second tenure, my role balanced between being the principal architect & business developer for the Azure business of this system integrator.

From the start of my career (2001), I served as an internal IT member for about 10 years. The roles over the years ranged from starting out as service engineer, towards the lead architect of revamping our custom built ERP (Mainframe) system and ending my tour-of-duty as an IT operations manager (7 direct reports)
